About LIRA

LIRA (Legal Intelligence & Research Assistant) is a standalone AI-powered legal tool built for individuals, law students, and professionals who need quick, structured legal guidance.

Our Mission

Access to legal knowledge should not be gated by expensive consultations. LIRA democratizes legal understanding through AI — giving everyone a starting point for research, document drafting, and understanding their rights.

What LIRA Can Do

  • Answer legal questions with structured, cited responses
  • Generate real legal documents (NDA, employment contracts, rental agreements, and more)
  • Save your research history for future reference
  • Work completely offline after setup — your data never leaves your machine

Technology

LIRA is built with Python (Flask), SQLite for local storage, and Google Gemini for AI capabilities. It runs entirely on your local machine — no cloud databases, no data sharing.
