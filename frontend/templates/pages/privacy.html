Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026

Data Storage

All your data — account information, research history, and drafted documents — is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your own machine. LIRA does not transmit your personal data to any external servers.

AI Queries

When you use Legal Research or Document Drafting, your queries are sent to Google Gemini API to generate responses. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. We recommend not including personally identifiable information in your queries.

API Keys

Your Gemini API key is stored in your browser session only and is never written to disk by LIRA. If you set it via environment variable, it remains on your local machine.

Passwords

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage. We never store plaintext passwords.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact page.
